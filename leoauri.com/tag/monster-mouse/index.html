Monster Mouse EP: Your Friend #204

May 18, 2018

Your Friend #204 consists of 4 songs packed with sounds and effects that will make your brain pop and your booty wiggle. While most Berliners were tucked into their cosy beds this winter, Monster Mouse boys Leo Auri, Nico Lengauer, and Jonathan Reiter were in the studio recording their first EP, a thoughtful experimentation with the boundaries of 2018 pop music.
